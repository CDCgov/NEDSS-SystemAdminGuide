NBS 7 is modular. Rather than a single fixed system, it is a set of components that you assemble based on the size, technical capacity, and public health needs of your jurisdiction. This section helps you understand your options and identify the right starting configuration for your jurisdiction.

Work through the following sections in order:

1. [Configuration tiers](1_choose_configuration/1_configuration_tiers/) — Understand the four NBS 7 configuration options
2. [Vendor-managed deployment](1_choose_configuration/2_vendor_managed_deployment/) — Guidance if your jurisdiction plans to use a vendor
3. [Decision tree](1_choose_configuration/3_decision_tree/) — Answer a series of questions to get a recommended starting configuration
